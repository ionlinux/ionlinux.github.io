We're excited to announce the Ion Linux project. Ion is a new Linux distribution focused on performance, simplicity, and security.

## Our Vision

Ion Linux aims to provide a fast, lightweight, and modern Linux experience without sacrificing usability. We believe your operating system should get out of the way and let you focus on what matters.

## What's Next

We're working hard on our first stable release. Stay tuned for:

- Installation ISO images
- Package repository
- Detailed documentation
- Community forums

Follow the project for updates and get involved in shaping the future of Ion Linux.
